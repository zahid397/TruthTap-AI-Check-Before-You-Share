# TruthTap AI — 3-Minute Pitch Video Script

**Format:** ~3 minutes · English (add English subtitles if you present in another language) · content matters most, keep it human and confident.
**Tip:** speak like someone who lives the problem, not like a textbook. Show the app, don't just describe it.

**Suggested timing:** Hook 0:00–0:20 · Problem 0:20–0:50 · Solution 0:50–1:15 · Demo 1:15–2:15 · Impact 2:15–2:45 · Close 2:45–3:00.

---

## 🎬 0:00 – 0:20 · OPENING HOOK
*(On camera, or over a fast montage of viral posts / a phone scrolling.)*

> “Three seconds. That's all it takes to hit *share* — and send something fake to a thousand people before anyone checks if it's true.
>
> Last year, a fake headline blamed a real country's blackout on sanctions. Millions saw it. It never happened. And the people who spread it? Mostly people our age.”

---

## ⚠️ 0:20 – 0:50 · THE PROBLEM
*(Simple on-screen stats as you say them.)*

> “Here's the uncomfortable truth. **76% of young people get their news from social media** — we share more than anyone. But we were never given the tools to check what we share.
>
> AI images look real. Deepfakes sound real. Fake screenshots of trusted news outlets spread faster than the corrections. And when 667 creators were asked their biggest challenge, the number one answer wasn't burnout or money — it was **misinformation and trust.**
>
> The problem isn't that we're careless. It's that in the three seconds before we share, we have *nothing* to help us stop and think.”

---

## 💡 0:50 – 1:15 · THE SOLUTION
*(Show the TruthTap AI home screen: “Check Before You Share.”)*

> “So we built **TruthTap AI** — a simple, free pause button for the internet.
>
> Four taps: **Paste. Check. Understand. Decide.**
>
> Paste anything you're unsure about, and TruthTap AI gives you a trust score, tells you *why* in plain language, traces the source, and shows you exactly what to do next. No login. No cost. Works on any phone.”

---

## 📱 1:15 – 2:15 · THE DEMO
*(Screen-record the real app. This is the heart of the video — let it breathe.)*

> “Let me show you. Here's a post going around:
> *‘BREAKING: All exams cancelled tomorrow — share now before they delete it!’*
>
> I paste it, tap **Analyze**…”

*(Result appears.)*

> “**Trust Score: 35 out of 100 — High Risk.** And look — it tells me *why*: urgent language, a share-now demand, a sensitive claim, and no source. Then it tells me what to do: don't share yet, check official sources, ask a teacher.
>
> I can even open **Source Trace** — no original source, no trusted match. Red flags everywhere.
>
> Now watch a real one — a news story *with* a Reuters link…”

*(Analyze the trusted sample.)*

> “**100 — Safe.** It found a trusted source. That's the difference, made visible in one tap.
>
> And because media literacy is a skill, we made it fun.”

*(Open the MIL Quiz, answer one question.)*

> “Real or fake? Safe to share? AI or human? Every answer teaches you the tells — using *real* cases, like this AI-generated whale photo that fooled thousands. You learn to spot the fakes yourself.”

---

## 🌍 2:15 – 2:45 · IMPACT
*(Back on camera.)*

> “Here's what makes TruthTap AI different: it doesn't just give you an answer — it *teaches* you. Every check is a lesson. The goal is for you to get so good at spotting misinformation that one day, you won't even need us.
>
> It's free forever, it runs on any phone, and a teacher can use it in a classroom tomorrow. One stopped share can protect a thousand people. That's the ripple we're building.”

---

## 🎯 2:45 – 3:00 · CLOSING LINE
*(Strong, direct, to camera.)*

> “Misinformation spreads because people share before they think. TruthTap AI flips that — one tap at a time.
>
> **Think first. Verify always. Share responsibly.**
>
> TruthTap AI. Check before you share.”

---

### 🎥 Production notes
- **Record the demo live** in the deployed app — real product beats slides every time.
- Keep energy up; this is a youth project — sound like a human who cares, not a corporate ad.
- On-screen text should reinforce (stats, the score numbers, the four words), never compete with your voice.
- End on the logo + tagline + your live URL.
